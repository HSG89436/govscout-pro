## Official Basis

- SAM.gov states that an entity seeking to apply directly for federal awards as a prime awardee needs a registration, and active registration must be maintained: https://sam.gov/content/duns-uei
- SBA explains that prime contractors work directly with the government and are responsible for contract performance: https://www.sba.gov/federal-contracting/contracting-guide/prime-subcontracting
- SBA states that SBA VetCert certified SDVOSBs may compete for federal SDVOSB set-aside and sole-source opportunities: https://www.sba.gov/federal-contracting/contracting-assistance-programs/veteran-contracting-assistance-programs
- SAM.gov is the official contract-opportunity search source: https://sam.gov/content/opportunities
