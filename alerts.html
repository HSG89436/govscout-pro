Lead Scoring

Cert Match

The set-aside matches your certification. This is the highest-priority lead — bid it directly.

🟡 TEAM

No set-aside — any business can compete. Good for teaming or subcontracting with a prime.

Cert-mismatch leads (e.g., 8(a) only when you're SDVOSB) are automatically filtered out — not sent.
