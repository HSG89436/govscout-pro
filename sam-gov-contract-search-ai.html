// FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need a SAM.gov API key to search?
▾
GovScout Pro uses a shared SAM.gov API key by default — you don't need to provide one to get started. If you want higher search rate limits, you can add your own free SAM.gov key from sam.gov/profile/details. It takes about 2 minutes to generate.
How is this different from searching SAM.gov directly?
▾
SAM.gov returns raw listings with no fit scoring. GovScout Pro adds your profile — your certifications, NAICS codes, and service areas — and scores each result before you see it. Instead of reading 50 listings to find 3 worth pursuing, you see your GO opportunities immediately.
What certifications does GovScout Pro score for?
▾
SDVOSB, VOSB, 8(a), WOSB, EDWOSB, HUBZone, Small Disadvantaged Business (SDB), and general SBA small business. Tell Marcus which certifications your company holds and he filters and scores accordingly.
Does GovScout Pro submit bids for me?
▾
No. GovScout Pro helps you find and analyze opportunities, build compliance checklists, and draft bid materials. Actual bid submission happens through your company's SAM.gov account and the awarding agency's designated submission system.
Is the SAM.gov data current?
▾
GovScout Pro pulls live data directly from the SAM.gov API. Data is current within SAM.gov's own update cycle (typically same-day for new postings). Always verify deadlines and amendments directly on SAM.gov before submitting anything.
