▶ NOW — May 2026
Intelligence Automation
▶
Sources Sought Interceptor
Monitor your saved NAICS codes and fire an alert the moment a Sources Sought or RFI posts — before the RFP exists. Marcus drafts your response automatically. This is where 80% of contracts are decided and most contractors miss it entirely.
▶
Email Deadline Alerts
Get notified when tracked contracts hit 72 hours, 7 days, and 30 days out. Marcus includes a specific action item in each alert — not just a reminder, a move.
▶
Incumbent Hunter
Automatic cross-reference against USASpending.gov for every contract — who has held it, how long, what they charged. Marcus tells you if this is worth fighting or already locked.
▶
CO Outreach Templates
Marcus writes the actual email to the contracting officer — specific to the contract, written in your voice, referencing their recent awards. One click to have a draft ready before competitors wake up.
▶
Proposal Section Scorer v2
Paste any section of your draft proposal and Marcus scores it against the evaluation criteria in real time. Rewrites weak sections on demand. Every sentence earns points or gets cut.
→ NEXT — June–July 2026
Platform & Intelligence
○
Real Competitor Intelligence
Pull SAM.gov and USASpending data to build an actual list of companies that have won similar contracts in your NAICS and set-aside category in the last 3 years. Marcus tells you their pricing strategy and how to beat them specifically.
○
Agency Budget Calendar — Live Data
Real fiscal year spending data by agency. Marcus tells you which agencies are burning budget right now and when each agency's spending window opens and closes based on actual award history.
○
Past Performance Builder
Turn commercial and utility industry experience into federal-equivalent past performance narratives using CPARS language. Track every project as past performance and build your federal record from day one.
○
GAO Protest Intelligence
Before you bid, Marcus checks the GAO protest database for that agency and contract type. If you lose unfairly, Marcus tells you if your bid is protest-worthy and walks you through the filing process.
○
NAICS Optimizer — Live Competition Data
Real competition density and SDVOSB count per NAICS code. Marcus recommends where to focus based on actual award data — stop competing where 800 other SDVOSBs are fighting for the same contracts.
○
Subcontractor Finder — SAM.gov Integration
Search real registered SAM.gov companies matching the specific NAICS and scope of each contract. Marcus builds the outreach message for each one.
◇ LATER — Q3–Q4 2026
Full Platform
◇
User Accounts + Cloud Storage
Save your business profile, pipeline, documents, and bid packages to the cloud. Access from any device. Today everything lives in your browser — this unlocks the full multi-device experience.
◇
True Daily Intelligence Briefing
Marcus sends you a morning briefing by email every day — new contracts matching your profile, deadline warnings, Sources Sought alerts, and one specific action he has identified that most contractors will miss today.
◇
Team Accounts
Multiple users on one account. BD team, proposal writer, and principal all working in the same pipeline with shared documents and Marcus available to everyone.
◇
White-Label for GovCon Consultants
Let GovCon consultants run GovScout Pro under their own brand for their clients. Marcus as a service — the consultant brings the relationship, Marcus brings the intelligence.
◇
Win/Loss Analytics Dashboard
After enough bid cycles Marcus identifies your patterns automatically. Which agencies you win at. Which set-aside categories perform. What your average win probability is by contract type. The data that turns guessing into strategy.

Marcus Mobile App
Full Marcus on iOS and Android. Review contracts, upload documents with your camera, get push notifications on deadlines and Sources Sought alerts. GovCon intelligence in your pocket.
