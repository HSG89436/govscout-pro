Privacy Policy

GovScout Pro  ·  Last updated: May 2026  ·  Questions: sharriman2002@gmail.com

Short version: GovScout Pro is designed to keep your sensitive data out of our hands. Your AI keys, documents, and business profile stay in your browser. We only receive what's necessary to process your subscription and deliver contract alerts.

1. Who We Are

GovScout Pro is operated by Harriman Solution Group, a service-disabled veteran-owned small business (SDVOSB) based in Reno, Nevada. CAGE Code: 9YZ59. Contact: sharriman2002@gmail.com.

2. What We Collect

Subscription and billing

When you subscribe at $9/month, PayPal processes your payment. PayPal shares your name and email address with us to confirm your subscription status. We do not receive or store your credit card or bank account details — PayPal holds those.

Alert subscriptions

If you sign up for contract alerts at govscout.pro/alerts, we store the following in a secure, access-controlled database:

  • Your name (optional)
  • Your email address
  • Your keywords, NAICS codes, and certification preferences
  • Your Telegram Chat ID (optional, if you want Telegram alerts)
  • Your alert frequency preference (daily or weekly)
  • A secure unsubscribe token tied to your email

This data is used only to run your automated contract scans and deliver alerts. We do not sell, rent, or share it.

What we do NOT collect

  • AI API keys — Your OpenAI, Anthropic, Groq, or Gemini key is stored only in your browser's localStorage. It is never transmitted to GovScout Pro's servers.
  • SAM.gov API key — Same as above. Stays in your browser.
  • Uploaded documents — When you upload a solicitation or capability statement, your browser sends it directly to your chosen AI provider. GovScout Pro's servers never see, receive, or store your documents.
  • Business profile data — Your Marcus profile (company details, certifications, past performance) is stored in your browser's localStorage only.
  • Chat history — Your conversations with Marcus are stored in your browser only.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To confirm your subscription and grant app access
  • To run daily and weekly SAM.gov contract scans on your behalf (alert subscribers only)
  • To deliver contract alert emails and Telegram messages (alert subscribers only)
  • To respond to support requests

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

  • PayPal — Subscription billing. Governed by PayPal's Privacy Policy.
  • Netlify — Hosting and serverless functions. Governed by Netlify's Privacy Policy.
  • Resend — Email delivery for contract alert notifications.
  • Telegram — Optional push alerts via the GovScoutProAlertsBot. Governed by Telegram's Privacy Policy.
  • SAM.gov — Federal contract data source. Public data only, no PII shared.
  • Your chosen AI provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, Groq, Google Gemini) — When you use Marcus, your browser sends queries directly to your provider. That provider's privacy policy governs how they handle those requests.

5. Data Retention

Alert subscriber records are retained until you unsubscribe. To remove your data, click the unsubscribe link in any alert email or contact us at sharriman2002@gmail.com. We will delete your record within 5 business days.

Browser-stored data (API keys, profile, chat history) is deleted when you clear your browser's localStorage or use the app's built-in clear function.

6. Security

Alert data is stored with access controlled by server-side tokens. The application is served over HTTPS. API keys and documents are never transmitted to our servers by design.

If you believe your data has been compromised, contact us immediately at sharriman2002@gmail.com.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold. Contact us at sharriman2002@gmail.com. We respond within 5 business days.

8. Children

GovScout Pro is a business tool for federal contractors. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes, we will update the date at the top and notify active alert subscribers by email. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.
