// FAQ

Common Questions

What kind of businesses can use GovScout?
▾
Any small business registered or eligible to register on SAM.gov. IT firms, consulting companies, design agencies, training organizations, staffing companies, construction contractors, widget manufacturers — all of them. Tell Marcus what your business does in plain English and he figures out the contracting strategy from there.
I don't have any certifications. Can Marcus still help me?
▾
Yes. Marcus can help identify programs that may match the facts you provide, explain what each program is used for, and direct you to the official eligibility and application steps. Eligibility is determined by the relevant agency or program authority.
What documents should I upload from SAM.gov?
▾
Everything. The solicitation document, Statement of Work, Performance Work Statement, any amendments, Q&A documents, attachments, and pricing templates. Marcus reads all of it. The more you upload the better his analysis. PDF, DOCX, and TXT formats all work.
What is a SAM.gov API key and how do I get one?
▾
SAM.gov is the official federal contract database. An API key is a free access code that lets GovScout search it automatically. You generate it at sam.gov/profile/details in about 2 minutes. Log in, scroll to API Keys, click Generate Key. Paste it into GovScout once and you're done.
Which AI key should I use?
▾
Marcus needs an AI key to analyze documents and answer questions. Best quality: Claude at console.anthropic.com (free credits for new accounts). Completely free: Groq at console.groq.com. Also works with GPT-4 (OpenAI) and Gemini (Google). Your key stays in your browser — never sent to GovScout.
How is GovScout different from other contracting tools?
▾
Most tools give you a search interface and call it AI. GovScout gives you Marcus — a consultant who knows your business, reads your documents, finds your subcontractors, and builds your bid package. He works for any small business type, handles every certification, and explains everything in plain English. You bring your own AI key — no markup, no data interception.
Is my data safe?
▾
Yes. GovScout has no backend server. Everything — your API keys, business profile, pipeline data — stays in your browser's local storage. When you search SAM.gov your browser talks directly to SAM.gov. When Marcus analyzes, your browser talks directly to your chosen AI provider. GovScout never sees or stores your data.
