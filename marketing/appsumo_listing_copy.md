## One-Sentence Pitch

## Short Description

Federal contracting can overwhelm a small team before it ever submits a response. GovScout.pro brings opportunity research and AI-assisted preparation together so you can focus on relevant notices, understand key requirements, and start draft materials without building a full business-development department.

## The Problem

SAM.gov has essential opportunity information, but small businesses still need to determine fit, read complex documentation, track next steps, and decide whether to invest in a response. Generic AI alone does not organize that process around a real pursuit.

## The Solution

GovScout.pro gives you a structured workspace for federal opportunity research. Use your business context to investigate opportunities, review solicitation materials with Marcus AI, identify key items that require attention, and prepare editable draft content for your team's review.

## Key Benefits

- Research SAM.gov opportunities within a focused small-business workflow
- Use AI assistance to summarize solicitation requirements in plainer language
- Add business and certification context to guide opportunity review
- Start editable draft materials and checklists for promising pursuits
- Save research and use available alert tools to support follow-up
- Keep human review at the center of every compliance and submission decision

## Suggested FAQ for AppSumo

### Is this only for certified businesses?

No. GovScout is built for small businesses pursuing federal work, whether they currently hold a certification or are still exploring the landscape. Official eligibility and certification decisions remain with government authorities.

### Does GovScout submit bids for me?

No. It assists with research and working drafts. You review and submit through the official process identified in the opportunity notice.

### Where does opportunity information come from?

The product is designed to support research using SAM.gov opportunity information. Always verify a notice, its amendments, and submission instructions on SAM.gov.

### Does Marcus AI guarantee compliance or an award?

No. Marcus AI helps summarize and draft; it is not a guarantee of compliance, eligibility, or award.
