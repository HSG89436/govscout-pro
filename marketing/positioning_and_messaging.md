# GovScout.pro Launch Positioning and Messaging

**Status:** Marketing source of truth for SaaS and AppSumo launch  
**Audience:** Small businesses exploring or pursuing federal opportunities  
**Offer shown on current site:** `$9/month`, cancel anytime; confirm offer and AppSumo deal terms before publishing campaign assets

## Positioning Statement

GovScout.pro helps small businesses turn federal opportunity research into a practical next step. Users can search SAM.gov opportunities, organize relevant pursuits, and use Marcus AI to understand solicitation materials and draft working bid content in plain English.

## One-Line Value Proposition

Find relevant federal opportunities, understand the documents, and prepare your next move with AI guidance built for small businesses.

## Message Hierarchy

1. **Find better-fit opportunities faster.** Start with live SAM.gov opportunity research rather than sifting through broad results alone.
2. **Understand what an opportunity requires.** Translate dense solicitation documents into clear requirements, questions, and action items.
3. **Move from research to preparation.** Draft working materials and checklists that your team can review and refine.
4. **Built for lean teams.** Give owners and small business development teams a structured workflow without a large capture operation.

### Short Description

GovScout.pro is a federal opportunity research and preparation workspace for small businesses. Bring your company context and your documents; Marcus AI helps you make sense of requirements and develop working drafts while you keep control of every decision and submission.

## Tone and Trust Rules

- Be direct, practical, and respectful of the complexity of federal contracting.
- Position AI as an assistant for research and drafts, not a contracting officer, attorney, certifying authority, or guarantee of award.
- Do not promise wins, award amounts, compliance, complete document coverage, or guaranteed eligibility.
- Attribute opportunity data to SAM.gov where relevant.
- Never state privacy, security, uptime, alert-delivery, or key-storage claims unless engineering and QA have verified them for production.
- Clearly state that users remain responsible for eligibility, compliance, pricing, final submissions, and official SAM.gov information.

## Standard Disclaimer

GovScout.pro provides software tools and AI-assisted drafts for opportunity research and preparation. It does not guarantee eligibility, compliance, awards, or outcomes. Always verify opportunity details on SAM.gov and review final submissions with qualified professionals when appropriate.
