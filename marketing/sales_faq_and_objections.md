# Sales FAQ and Objection Handling

**Purpose:** Keep website, AppSumo replies, email support, and founder-led sales conversations clear and consistent.

## Sales Summary

### Elevator Pitch

GovScout.pro is a research and preparation workspace for small businesses pursuing federal opportunities. It brings SAM.gov opportunity research together with Marcus AI assistance so users can understand documents and develop working drafts while retaining control of final decisions and submissions.

### 30-Second Version

Federal opportunity research takes time, and small teams can lose days interpreting documents before they know whether a pursuit is a fit. GovScout helps them research opportunities, summarize solicitation materials, and start reviewed draft content from one workflow. It is designed to support human judgment, not promise awards or replace official sources.

### Conversion CTA

Start by creating your business profile and researching one opportunity relevant to your capabilities.

## Objection Responses

### "I can search SAM.gov for free."

You should continue using SAM.gov as the official source. GovScout is designed for the work after and around the search: applying your business context, making documents easier to review, organizing next steps, and beginning editable drafts.

### "AI cannot understand my bid."

AI should not be the final reviewer of a federal response. GovScout uses AI as a preparation aid: to summarize content, surface questions, and create a starting draft. Your subject-matter knowledge and final verification remain essential.

### "I am not certified, so federal work is not for me."

Many small businesses pursue federal opportunities without a set-aside certification. GovScout can support research, but official eligibility and the right contracting strategy should be verified through authoritative sources and appropriate advisors.

### "I cannot risk missing a compliance requirement."

That is exactly why all AI output must be treated as a working aid, not a compliance certification. Confirm every requirement, amendment, form, representation, deadline, and submission instruction in the official notice before submitting.

### "How do I know my data is protected?"

We should provide a precise, written explanation of data handling after it is confirmed in the production system and privacy policy. Until that confirmation is complete, do not make broader assurances than documented facts.

### "Why buy this on AppSumo instead of waiting?"

Approved response after terms are set: AppSumo purchasers can access the defined deal tier under the published redemption and update terms. The deal page should state exactly what is included so purchasers can choose on facts, not urgency.

### "Will this get me a contract?"

No software can guarantee an award. GovScout aims to help you spend your time on better-informed research and preparation, with final decisions made by you and awards made by agencies.

## High-Risk Claims To Reject

Do not publish or repeat:

- "Find contracts you can win."
- "Win before the RFP is written."
- "Complete bid package ready to submit."
- "Guarantees compliance" or "never miss an opportunity."
- Unverified claims about founder credentials, agency experience, certifications, agency spend, storage, encryption, security, or data handling.
- Any implication that GovScout is government-endorsed or that AI is a contracting official.
