## FAQ

### Is GovScout a replacement for SAM.gov?

No. SAM.gov remains the official source for federal opportunity notices and submission instructions. GovScout is a research and preparation workspace that helps you work through relevant information.

### Does GovScout guarantee that I will win a contract?

No. GovScout assists with research, summaries, and working drafts. Contract outcomes depend on eligibility, fit, compliance, competition, pricing, evaluation, and agency decisions.

### Can I use GovScout if I do not hold a set-aside certification?

Yes. Small businesses can use the workflow to research opportunities and understand requirements. Any certification guidance should be verified with the appropriate agency or advisor.

### What should I review before submitting anything?

Confirm the official notice and amendments on SAM.gov, check eligibility and representations, validate all pricing and claims, and review every generated draft before submission.

### Are AI-generated drafts final proposal documents?

No. They are working drafts intended to accelerate your review and preparation process. You remain responsible for final content and submission.
